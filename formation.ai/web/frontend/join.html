Why Formation?

How we work

Our mission is to use data to serve human motivation.

Since 2015, we’ve been hard at work building a platform that fundamentally enhances the way in which customers are able to interact with the brands they love. That work has just begun.

As we continue the pursuit of our mission, we are looking to build a world-class team of individuals, all of whom will drive impact in a meaningful way.

Culture

Our Culture Includes Everyone.

At Formation we believe that diversity, equity, and inclusion are essential to our success. We are committed to ensuring Formation is a fair and welcoming place to work for all, where we celebrate the myriad of perspectives, backgrounds and experiences of our team members. We embrace each other as the unique individuals we are.

Above all, we foster inclusive environments in order to do the best work of our lives.

Benefits

We invest in the health & well-being of our team

Health

100% medical, dental, and vision premiums paid for full-time employees and eligible dependents.

Commuter Benefits

We assist you with getting to and from work, including pre-tax transit benefits or a pre-tax parking benefit.

Discretionary PTO

We appreciate work-life balance and have a discretionary PTO policy in addition to 12 paid holidays per year.

Wellness

We invest in your well-being with our Wellness Reimbursement Program. Employees have access to meditation and mindfulness resources.

Family Friendly

We are family-oriented and provide paid parental leave for those growing their families

Growth & Development

We offer company-wide trainings and an internal mentorship program.

Employee Spotlight

Erica Sabalones / HR Manager

What do you find most interesting about the work you are doing?

The most interesting aspect of the work I do is interacting with brilliant people. Our employees are exceptional at what they do and they have incredible backgrounds and interests.

What motivates your work, what keeps you going?

One of our company values is “We are always learning”. This resonates with my day to day work. I am motivated to continuously iterate, reinvent and improve how I do things at Formation.

Erica Sabalones / HR Manager

What was a major selling point for you to consider working at Formation?

Flexibility and the types of data and projects you have the opportunity to work on/with.

How has Formation promoted your professional development?

By being given many, many opportunities to speak to and with clients I have become better with client communication and developed my presentation skills further.
